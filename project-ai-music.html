AI-Generated Music Project

Exploring AI creativity through music generation and graphic design during exchange program at Artveldehs, Ghent

About This Project

This project was completed during my exchange program at Artvelde University of Applied Sciences in Ghent, Belgium. It represents a collaborative exploration of AI-driven creativity, combining advanced prompting techniques with artistic expression.

The assignment focused on harnessing the power of AI tools like Google's Gemini and Suno AI to create original music, while simultaneously demonstrating graphic design skills through the visual presentation and branding of the musical work. This project showcases my ability to effectively prompt AI systems, understand their capabilities and limitations, and integrate AI-generated content into cohesive creative projects.

Key Features

  • Advanced AI prompting techniques with Gemini
  • Music generation using Suno AI
  • Creative graphic design and visual identity
  • Team collaboration in international setting
  • Integration of AI tools into creative workflow
  • Documentation of prompting strategies and iterations

Skills Demonstrated

  • AI Prompt Engineering - Crafting effective prompts for music generation
  • Graphic Design - Creating visual identity for the musical project
  • Creative Problem Solving - Iterating and refining AI outputs
  • Collaboration - Working effectively in a diverse team environment
  • Cultural Adaptability - Thriving in an international academic setting
