Pheme News

Precision Market & Tech Intelligence - Get exclusive AI and finance news quickly, free of distraction

About This Project

Pheme News is a web application designed to provide exclusive AI and finance news quickly and efficiently, without distractions. The platform delivers precision market and tech intelligence, focusing on curated, high-quality content in the AI and finance sectors.

Built with a focus on user experience and information delivery, Pheme News aggregates and presents news in a clean, distraction-free interface. The platform monitors multiple sectors including AI, Finance, and US Politics, providing users with real-time intelligence that matters.

Currently in beta version, the platform is continuously being refined based on user feedback and evolving market needs. The application updates every 45 minutes, ensuring users stay informed with the latest developments in their areas of interest.

Key Features

  • Distraction-free news interface
  • Exclusive AI and finance news aggregation
  • Real-time sector monitoring (AI, Finance, US Politics)
  • Fast-loading, optimized performance
  • Clean, minimal design focused on content
  • Regular updates (every 45 minutes)
  • Precision market and tech intelligence

Project Goals

  • Provide quick access to relevant news without clutter
  • Focus on quality over quantity in news delivery
  • Create a streamlined user experience for professionals
  • Deliver actionable intelligence in AI and finance sectors
